About Me

Hi, Everyone!

I'm Mekayla - the ATL Foodie creator.

I live in Atlanta, my hometown. I am a foodie at heart and I believe food is the key to the soul. I've always enjoyed exploring new restaurants, discoving new cuisines, and growing my food palette whenever I travel. ATL in particular, my hometown, has so many great places to showcase. I wanted to highlight the amazing food and owners in Atlanta because I believe we don't receive enough love. I hope you enjoy my blog and find some amazing new places to try while you are in the city!

I hope you love what I have to offer here!

Feel free to reach out with comments or suggestions!
