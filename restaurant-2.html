Maggiano's Little Italy

A Must try Italian Restaurant

Tuscan Mussels

Although a franchise, Maggiano’s Little Italy provides an authentic Italian feeling. It transports you into a realm filled with rich Mediterranean flavours.


I highly recommend the Tuscan Mussels as an appretiser. And if you want pasta on the healthier side, you can try the Rigatoni Arrabbiata which has grilled chicken, spinach, and a tomato cream sauce with a little kick.
