Kinotori Ramen Bar

The Best Ramen in Atlanta

Fried Shrimp Bao buns and Honey Miso Ramen with fried shrimp

Kinotori Ramen Bar is the best Ramen in Atlanta, located right across the street from Ponce City Market. Ramen is served in large bowls, providing optimal room for plenty of noodles, meat, and vegetables. Compared to its competitors, Kinotori is affordably priced, offering a range variety of food on their menu. They can also accomodate most dietary restrictions, including options for vegetarians and vegans. If you decide to stop by and try them out, make sure to try my personal facorite appetizer, Bao buns (soft buns). You won't be disappointed!
